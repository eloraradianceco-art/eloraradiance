How It Works

Seven Sections Every Week

Each week has a key theme and seven study sections. Work through one each day and you've completed the week by Sunday. Structured on purpose — because consistency is the discipline.

📖
Scripture
The key passage for the week — read, sit with it, and let it anchor the rest of the study.
📚
Study
Deep theological notes with background context, Verse Map commentary, and cross-references.
🪞
Reflect
Guided reflection questions to move truth from your head to your heart and into your actual life.
⚡
Apply
A specific challenge for the week — not generic encouragement, but a concrete thing to do differently.
🙏
Prayer
A prayer written directly from the week's theme — specific, scriptural, and honest.
📝
Journal
Your personal space to write responses, questions, and what God is showing you. Saved to your account.
📊
Week End
A closing summary and check-in for the week — what landed, what still needs work, what's next.
