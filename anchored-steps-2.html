What's Inside

Go Deeper.
Stay the Course.

📖
Passage

Each week opens with the key passage for that theme — presented clearly with full context and readability. The Word first, always.

🎯
Context

Year 2 goes deeper into the background of each passage — historical context, original audience, and what it meant before what it means to you now.

⚠️
Don't Miss

The most important insight of the week — the one thing you cannot walk away without. Curated so nothing essential gets lost in the volume.

🧠
Study

Full theological and practical study notes for each week's theme. Year 2 is built for believers ready to move past milk and into meat.

💭
Reflect & Apply

Guided reflection and application sections that take Scripture from the page into your actual week — specific, honest, and actionable.

🙏
Prayer

A written prayer for the week's theme plus personal prayer space. Both the guided word and your own — side by side every week.

📊
Tracker

Track your weekly engagement across all 52 weeks. Consistency is visible, which builds the habit. See where you've been and what's ahead.

🌿
Community

Year 2 has a community tab — because the deeper you go in Scripture, the more you need others on the same path walking alongside you.
