How It Works

Six Layers of Study
on Every Passage

Each passage opens into a complete study experience — not a devotional that skims the surface, but a full encounter with what Jesus said and meant.

📖
The Passage
The full red-letter text — every word Jesus spoke in that passage, exactly as recorded, with nothing cut for brevity.
🧭
Context
Where He was, who He was talking to, and what had just happened. The setting shapes the meaning. You get the full picture.
💡
Meaning
What Jesus meant — unpacking the theology, the original context, and what was at stake in what He said.
⚡
Live It
Specific, honest application — not generic encouragement, but a real question about what this passage demands of you today.
🙏
Pray It
A prayer written directly from the text — the content of what Jesus taught, spoken back to the Father in your own name.
📝
Journal
Write your responses, questions, and reflections. Saved to your account — private, persistent, accessible on any device.

Also Included

Everything You Need
to Go Deeper

🧠
Memorization Tools
Three modes — Read & Recall, Fill the Gaps, Write it Out — for every passage. The Word hidden in the heart is the sword always in your hand.
↗
Share Cards
Generate a beautiful image card from any passage or study section. Share the full passage, the prayer, or the meaning. Light and dark modes.
🔍
Search

☆
Save Passages
Star any passage for quick access. Your saved library is always available from the top navigation.

Export Your Journal

📊
Track Progress
See what you've read, what you've memorized, and what you've saved — your progress recorded in Settings.
