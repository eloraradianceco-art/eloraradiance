How It Works

Your Warfare Training
in 4 Steps

01

Choose Your Weapon

Select from 23 weapons on the home screen. Each one is a different area of spiritual life the enemy targets — and a different tool God has given you to fight back.

02

Study the Scripture & Teaching

Read the Scripture for that weapon, then go deep in the teaching. Understand the theology, the enemy's tactics, and the spiritual truth that underpins your authority.

03

Declare, Pray, and Journal

Speak the declaration aloud. Pray the warfare prayer. Write your personal response in the journal. This is where head knowledge becomes lived practice.

04

Train with the Tool

Each weapon has an interactive training tool — battle logs, stronghold breakers, memorization, trackers. Return to it daily. This is your long-term warfare discipline.
